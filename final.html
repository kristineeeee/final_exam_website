ABOUT US

Moonhouse is popular for its historical facts as the "Cradle of Noble Heroes.
But in addition to its tourism industry, is also known for being a prominent producer of meat products,
particularly beef.The city boasts several meat dishes,such as the popular beef caldereta, a thick spicy
stew made with tender beef, and Tapang Taal,which is a dish consisting of beef or pork parts marinated in a
mixture of soy sauce, calamansi, and garlic.Apart from its meat products, Tanauan is also renowned for its
delicious fish products.Visitors can indulge in a variety of mouth-watering seafood dishes, showcasing the
city's abundance of fresh fish.In short,Tanauan City is a food lover paradise,offering a diverse array of meat and
fish products that are sure to tantalize the taste buds of any visitor while relishing in the industrial park and landmarks skip.
